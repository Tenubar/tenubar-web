## Overview
**Stop! Game** is a real-time, multiplayer word game built with **React Native (Expo)** on the front end and **Node.js + MongoDB** on the back end. Players can register with email or Google, play as guests, create or join rooms, chat globally, and compete on leaderboards. The app focuses on smooth real-time sync, fair validation, and a polished cross-platform experience across mobile, web, and tablet layouts.

**Visit the Game Website:** https://playstop.onrender.com

**Visit the Game at Itch.io:** https://tenubar.itch.io/stop-the-game

---

## Key Features
1. **Real-time Multiplayer:** Socket.IO rooms for live gameplay, lobbies, and chat.
2. **Authentication + Guests:** JWT auth with email verification, Google OAuth, and guest sessions.
3. **Leaderboards:** Global, weekly, and friends rankings.
4. **Premium Access:** One-time payment unlock with Stripe and PayPal support.
5. **AI Validation:** OpenAI-assisted answer validation with fallback rules and resync logic.
6. **Cross-Platform UI:** Responsive layouts for mobile, web, and tablets with fixed action footers.

## Challenges and Learnings
1. **Real-time Sync:** Preventing missed STOP/round-end events required resync and validation retries.
2. **Session Safety:** Handling reconnects, duplicate sessions, and background disconnects to avoid ghost players.
3. **Language Enforcement:** Room-level language consistency across gameplay and AI validation.

---

## Final Thoughts
1. **Gameplay First:** Fast, clear gameplay flows are critical for multiplayer retention.
2. **Continuous Improvement:** We keep refining validation, UI polish, and reliability as the game grows.
